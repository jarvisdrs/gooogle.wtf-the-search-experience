## FOIA Request Guidelines

### What to Request
1. FBI case files (Epstein, Maxwell, associates)
2. BOP detention records
3. DOJ OIG investigation materials
4. State Department records (passport/travel)
5. Treasury/FinCEN financial records

### Where to Submit

**FBI:**
- Online: https://www.fbi.gov/services/information-management/foipa
- Address: FBI, Attn: FOI/PA Request, 170 Marcel Drive, Winchester, VA 22602

**DOJ:**
- Online: https://www.justice.gov/oip/foia-request

**BOP:**
- Online: https://www.bop.gov/foia/

**CIA (if applicable):**
- Online: https://www.cia.gov/readingroom/

### Processing Times
- Simple requests: 20 business days
- Complex requests: May take months to years
- Expedited processing available for media/public interest

### Fees
- First 100 pages: Free (recreational/educational)
- Standard: $0.15 per page
- Search fees may apply for complex requests
