## How to Access Court Documents

### PACER (Federal Courts)
- Website: https://www.pacer.gov/
- Cost: $0.10 per page (capped at $3.00 per document)
- Case numbers required
- Free for parties to the case

### CourtListener (Free Alternative)
- Website: https://www.courtlistener.com/
- Free access to many federal documents
- RECAP archive from PACER

### State Court Records
- Florida: https://www.mypalmbeachclerk.com/
- New York: https://iapps.courts.state.ny.us/webcivil/OCA#

## Key Legal Concepts

### Non-Prosecution Agreement (NPA)
- Agreement not to prosecute in exchange for cooperation or other terms
- Epstein's 2008 NPA was controversial and later challenged

### Crime Victims' Rights Act (CVRA)
- 18 U.S.C. ยง 3771
- Requires victims be notified of plea agreements
- Central to challenge of Epstein's 2008 deal

### Child Victims Act (New York)
- Opened look-back window for childhood sexual abuse claims
- Enabled multiple lawsuits against Epstein estate
