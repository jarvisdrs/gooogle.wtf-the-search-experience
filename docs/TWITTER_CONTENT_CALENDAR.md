## Content Types Mix

### Daily Breakdown
- **40%** Product screenshots/features
- **30%** Memes/reaction content
- **20%** Engagement (polls, questions)
- **10%** Behind the scenes/meta

### Engagement Tactics
- Reply to every mention within 2 hours
- Like tweets using #gooogle
- Retweet quality memes from community
- Quote tweet with witty responses

## Voice & Tone

- Self-aware
- Slightly unhinged but professional
- Degen culture references
- Never mean-spirited
- Always ironic, never misleading

## Don'ts

- No offensive content
- No political statements
- No actual harmful advice
- No spam hashtags
- No engagement bait (like for like)

## Analytics to Track

- Engagement rate (target: 5%+)
- Follower growth (target: 100/week)
- Click-through to site (use short links)
- Top performing content types
- Best posting times

## Tools

- **Scheduling**: TweetDeck (free) or Buffer
- **Analytics**: Twitter Analytics + Google Analytics
- **Design**: Canva for memes
- **Links**: bit.ly for tracking
